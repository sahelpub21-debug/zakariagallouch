Fan4All — SportTech Marketplace

Building a creator-led growth engine for fans, clubs & brands

Fan4All connects fans with licensed products, drops and club-driven experiences. I led a dual track: strategic communications (positioning, message house, launches) and content-to-commerce growth (creator ops, GTM, and community programs), aligning product, partners, and media.

Brief & Scope

Establish Fan4All as a credible SportTech marketplace and accelerate demand generation through creator partnerships, sport communities and product storylines tied to seasonal moments (fixtures, derbies, tournaments).

  • Message architecture & positioning (fans × clubs × brands).
  • Launch calendar: drops, collabs, gift guides, live match hooks.
  • Influencer/athlete seeding, UGC prompts, affiliate logic.
  • Analytics & learning loops (SKU heatmaps, content cohorts).

Strategy

  • From hype to habit: tie content to predictable weekly moments (fixtures) for compounding reach.
  • Fan Problems → Product Proof: frictionless purchase, authenticity, size/fit, shipping clarity.
  • Creator flywheel: briefs with hooks & CTAs; reuse clips across app, email, social, PDPs.
  • Trust signals: licensed marks, unboxing, care tips, club stories, return policy clarity.

Channels & Mechanics

  • Short-form video (9:16 & 1:1) with subtitles; live score hooks.
  • Editorial drops (kits, retro, limited editions) with scarcity logic.
  • Partner integrations: club pages, QR at venues, matchday screens.
  • Ambassadors & micro-influencers with affiliate tracking.

Video Pillars

  • Drop Trailers: 20–30s, fast hooks, product hero.
  • Creator Fits: try-ons, sizing, styling for matchday.
  • Fan Stories: user clips, rivalries, local club culture.

Commerce Helpers

  • Size & care guides embedded on PDPs.
  • “Compare kits” carousels with close-ups.
  • Localized copy & shipping times.

Partner Toolkits

  • Club/brand asset packs (logos, color tokens, CTAs).
  • Creator brief templates & legal checklist.
  • Post calendar + UTM presets.
